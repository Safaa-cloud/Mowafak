# Candidate Consent Notice

## AI-Assisted Interview Pre-Screening

**Important: Please read this notice carefully before proceeding.**

---

### What You Need to Know

You are participating in Mowafak's AI-assisted pre-screening interview process. This notice explains how your data will be used and your rights throughout the process.

### The Process

1. **Voice Recording**: You will be asked up to 5 questions and will record your answers using your browser's microphone.

2. **AI Transcription**: Your voice answers will be automatically transcribed to text using speech-to-text technology.

3. **AI Assessment**: An AI system will evaluate your transcribed answers based on the job requirements, generating scores for relevance, clarity, and technical depth.

4. **Human Review**: A qualified HR professional will review the AI's assessment along with your full transcript.

5. **Human Decision**: **All hiring decisions are made by humans.** The AI system cannot and will not automatically reject or advance any candidate.

### Your Data

| Data Type | Storage Duration | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Voice Recordings | 30 days | Transcription and quality assurance |
| Transcripts | 90 days | HR review and assessment |
| AI Assessment | 1 year | Audit and compliance |
| Personal Information | Per privacy policy | Application processing |

### Your Rights

- ✅ **You control your data**: You may request deletion of your recordings and transcripts at any time by contacting hr@company.com
- ✅ **Human decision guaranteed**: No automated decisions will be made about your application
- ✅ **Transparency**: You can request a copy of your AI assessment after the process is complete
- ✅ **Withdraw consent**: You can withdraw consent at any time without penalty to your application
- ✅ **Alternative process**: If you prefer not to use the AI-assisted process, contact HR for alternative arrangements

### How We Protect You

- **Bias Monitoring**: We regularly audit our AI system for bias across gender, ethnicity, and accent
- **Evidence-Based**: AI assessments must provide exact quotes from your answers as evidence
- **Audit Trail**: Every HR decision is permanently logged and can be audited
- **No Auto-Reject**: The system is designed to make automatic rejection **impossible**

### Data Security

- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized HR personnel only
- Audit logs track all access to candidate data

### Regulatory Compliance

This process complies with:
- NYC Automated Employment Decision Tools (AEDT) Law (Local Law 144)
- EU AI Act (High-Risk AI Systems Category)
- GDPR Article 22 (Automated Decision-Making)
- Egyptian Data Protection Law

### Contact

If you have questions about this process or wish to exercise your data rights:
- Email: privacy@company.com
- HR Department: hr@company.com
